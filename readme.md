### Positivus - Digital Marketing Agency Landing Page
A modern, responsive landing page for a digital marketing agency built with HTML and CSS.

### Overview
Positivus is a clean and professional landing page showcasing digital marketing services. The design emphasizes clarity, modern aesthetics, and user engagement with strategically placed calls-to-action.
Features

# Hero Section: Eye-catching headline with tagline emphasizing digital success
Services Showcase: Six core service offerings displayed in card format:

Search Engine Optimization
Pay-per-click Advertising
Social Media Marketing
Email Marketing
Content Creation
Analytics and Tracking


# Call-to-Action: Prominent CTA encouraging visitor engagement
Case Studies: Real-world examples demonstrating proven results
Working Process: Step-by-step guide section
Team Section: Meet the team behind the strategies
Testimonials: Social proof from satisfied clients
Contact Form: Easy connection point for potential clients
